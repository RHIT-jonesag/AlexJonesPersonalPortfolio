About Me

My name is Alex Jones and I am an aspiring computer scientist. I am currently 18 years old and I will start college in the fall of 2024. I plan to double major in software development & graphic design, and also minor in photography. I love taking photos and finding the beauty in the things around me. Along with photography, I spend my time by playing video games with my friends, playing volleyball, and playing dungeons and dragons. When I'm not playing games I like to code random projects, whether that be for helping a friend or just for myself. I was first introduced to programming in the summer of 2022, and I've loved it ever since. I've learned and practiced several different programming languages and found that web deveolpment was my niche. I hope to go into the field of web development and design and use my background to make stunning websites for others.

My First Projects

The Male Post

The Male Post is the first website I made during the summer of 2023. I created it to bring awareness to men's mental health which is an important topic to shed light on in today's culture which is why I chose this topic. Additionally, I designed the name and logo.

The Split Path

The Split Path is a "choose your own adventure" game that I coded as my second main project during the summer of 2023. The game presents you with two options on each screen that lead to several outcomes for the story.

What Kind of Meat Are You?

This Buzzfeed Quiz was the third main project I coded during the summer of 2023. Similar to The Split Path, in this, you answer two questions that give you an answer as to what kind of meat you are.
